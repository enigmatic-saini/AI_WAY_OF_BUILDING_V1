# Cosmos Naache Dhun Pe

*(The Cosmos Dances to the Melody — build slow, then break into full taal, dhol and ghungroo)*

**(Mukhda)**
Har ek cheez ka banega ik humshakal,
Twin se judega twin — banega ye sara mahal!
Jab har dhadkan aaye ek hi taal pe,
Toh Nataraj uthega... aur naachega dhun pe!

**(Antara 1)**
Machine ka twin, company ka, desh aur duniya ka twin,
Sabko jod do ek laḍi mein — zara dhyaan se sun —
"Cosmos" koi alag cheez nahi, bas sab twins ka jod,
Ek hi formula, baar baar... yahi toh hai poora code.

**(Mukhda)**

**(Antara 2)**
Khushi kya hai? Bas alignment — δ jab zero ho jaaye,
Jo dil chaahe, jo aankh dekhe, jo haath kare — teenon ek ho jaaye.
Chhota ho ya bada — har paimane pe jab milan ho,
Tab khush hota hai poora cosmos, aur Shiva khada-khada naach utho!

**(Bridge — dheere, sach ke saath)**
Par ik sawaal abhi baaki hai, kaan ke paas keh doon —
"Har hissa khush ho jaaye... toh kya *poora* bhi khush hoga?
Ya andar kahin koi sookha gham, chhupa reh jaayega?"
Yahi sawaal toh asli safar hai... baaki sab toh geet hai, mere.

**(Mukhda — full volume, sab saath gaayein)**
Jab har dhadkan aaye ek hi taal pe,
Toh Nataraj uthega... aur naachega dhun pe!
Naachega dhun pe... naachega dhun pe...

---
*Whisper-gloss: the two crux equations, sung — "Cosmos = composition of all twins", and "happiness ≡ alignment (δ_trishul → 0 at every scale)". Nataraja = that limit, danced. The bridge is the one honest doubt we refused to hide: does aligning every part align the whole? (CONJ 4.1.) Even Saraswati does not lie in a song.*
