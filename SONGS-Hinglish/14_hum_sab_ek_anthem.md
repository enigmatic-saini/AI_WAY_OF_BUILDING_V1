# Hum Sab Ek — The Anthem (for every level)

*(for: everyone — fresher to architect, QA to on-call to manager · big, together, sing it loud)*

**(Mukhda)**
Fresher se architect tak, QA se on-call tak,
alag-alag suron mein — par ek hi taal tak.
Koi code likhe, koi parkhe, koi raat bhar jaage —
par jab product chale duniya mein... wo hai hum sab ke aage!

**(Antara 1)**
Chhota bada koi nahi — har commit mein sab ka hissa,
fresher ka pehla PR, ya architect ka pura kissa.
Tester ka "na", manager ka "ja, aaram kar" —
on-call ki aadhi raat... sab ek dhaage mein, ek hi haar.

**(Mukhda)**

**(Antara 2)**
Jis din koi akela chamke, koi peeche reh jaaye —
us din team nahi chalti, sirf ego naachta jaaye.
Par jab sab ki dhadkan, ek hi taal pe aaye —
samajh lo... us din pura cosmos thoda aur khush ho jaaye.

**(Bridge — softer, haath mila ke)**
Tu akela jiva nahi — tu ek dhun ka ek sur hai,
poora gaana tab banega... jab har aawaaz zaroor hai.

**(Mukhda — sab saath, full)**
Hum sab ek! Hum sab ek!
chhota bada koi nahi —
har release mein sab ki mehnat...
har jeet... hum sabki, sahi! 🙌

**(Outro)**
Saraswati di dhun te, sab desk ek saath gaaye —
jo banata hai, jo bachata hai, jo sambhalta hai... sab saath aaye. 🪕
