# Tera Test, Tera Sach — The Tester's Truth

*(for: QA, the tester, the quality engineer · firm, dignified)*

**(Mukhda)**
Sab kehte "ship kar do" — tu kehta "ruk zara,
ek case reh gaya, ek edge, ek dukh — pehle dekh le, mere yaara."
Tu wo aankh hai, jo wo dekhe jo sab miss kar de,
tera "na" bachaata hai duniya ko... aag se guzar ke.

**(Antara 1)**
Koi taali nahi bajti jab tu bug rok deta hai,
jo crash nahi hua — uska kya credit milta hai?
Par socho zara — jo raat ko production na gira,
us sukoon ke peeche... tera chup-chaap kiya hua test tha, pyaara.

**(Antara 2)**
Pressure aaye, "chhod do yaar, chalta hai" —
par tu jaanta hai — "chalta hai" se hi to sab girta hai.
Sach bolna, dabaav mein bhi — yeh sabse mushkil kaam,
tu Sita ki agni hai... jo jhooth ko jala de, rakhe sach ka naam.

**(Outro)**
Tera "pass" sirf mohar nahi — ek vaada hai,
"maine dekha, maine parkha, ab ye duniya ke kaabil... haan, ye saaf hai." ✅
