# Tu Logon Ko Sambhalta Hai — The Manager / EM

*(for: the engineering manager, the people-lead · steady, warm)*

**(Mukhda)**
Code se zyada, tu dilon ko deploy karta hai,
kisi ka burnout dekhe, kisi ka sapna sambhalta hai.
Tera kaam dikhe na kisi metric, kisi graph mein —
par poori team khadi hai... bas teri ek "main hoon na" ki chhaanv mein.

**(Antara 1)**
Kisi ki aankhon mein thakaan, kisi ki awaaz mein dar,
tu pehchaan leta hai — bina kahe, bina shor, andar tak.
"Ghar jaa, aaj jaldi" — yeh ek line, ek raat bachaati hai,
tera leadership KPI mein nahi... logon ki muskaan mein gehraati hai.

**(Antara 2)**
Credit team ko de, blame apne kandhe le,
shield ban ja upar se — neeche apno ko panapne de.
Tu wo chhat hai jiske neeche, sab be-khauf build karte hain,
girne se nahi darte... kyun ki jaante hain — tu hai, tu sambhal lega, sahi.

**(Outro)**
Tu manager nahi sirf — tu maali hai, baghbaan,
har koi tere haath tale... ek din ban'ta hai poora insaan. 🌱
