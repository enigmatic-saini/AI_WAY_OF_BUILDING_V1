# Raah Dikha — The Lead & Architect

*(for: the senior engineer, the tech lead, the architect · expansive, generous)*

**(Mukhda)**
Ab sirf apna code nahi — poori team ki raah,
upar se dekh, door tak dekh, phir kheench laqeer saaf.
Junior gire toh haath de, naya raasta bana —
tu architect hai, dost... pul banata hai, deewar nahi, zara sun na.

**(Antara 1)**
Yaad hai? Tu bhi kabhi dara hua fresher tha,
aaj jo tujhe aata hai — kisi ne sikhaaya tha.
Ab teri baari hai — wo deepak aage badha,
tera sabse bada code... wo log hain, jinhe tune khada kiya, suna?

**(Antara 2)**
"Nahi" bolna seekh, jab scope phoolne lage,
desire ko chhota mat kar — par sapna asli rakh, aage.
Design soch, tradeoff likh, alternatives bhi naam —
ek perfect teer chala... Lakshmana wali, ek hi nishaan, ek hi kaam.

**(Outro)**
Sabse aage chal, par akela mat ho,
tu raah dikha... aur peeche mud ke, sabko saath le lo. 🧭
