# Naya Naya — The Fresher's Anthem

*(for: the intern, the fresher, the first-job junior · warm, uplifting)*

**(Mukhda)**
Naya naya hai, sab kuch naya,
samajh na aaye? Koi baat nahi, bhaiya.
Har badaa expert, kabhi fresher tha —
tera bhi waqt aayega... bas seekhta jaa, seekhta jaa.

**(Antara 1)**
Screen pe error laal, dil mein thodi ghabraahat,
"sabko sab aata, mujhe kuch nahi" — yeh jhooth hai, sun le baat.
Sabne yahi socha tha, pehle din, dare-dare,
phir ek din samajh aaya — sab seekh ke hi to aaye, yaar mere.

**(Antara 2)**
Pooch lena senior se, sawaal chhota ho ya bada,
sharminda mat ho — na poochna hi galti hai, dost zara suna.
Aaj tu copy-paste karta hai, kal tujhse log seekhenge,
yeh chhoti si shuruaat hai... ek din tere naam pe build chalega!

**(Outro)**
Dheere dheere, line by line,
tu ban jaayega — bas tu lagaa reh, sab theek hai, sab fine.
