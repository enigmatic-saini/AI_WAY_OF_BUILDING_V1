# Roz Ka Build — The Builder's Song

*(for: the software engineer, the developer · steady, proud)*

**(Mukhda)**
Roz subah, ek nayi commit,
bug aaya toh fix, fail hua toh re-submit.
Chhoti chhoti lines mein, duniya banti hai —
tu builder hai, yaar... teri keyboard pe kainaat chalti hai.

**(Antara 1)**
Koi naam na jaane tera, par app crore log chalaayein,
tu peeche khada, chup-chaap — duniya teri likhi cheez pe muskuraaye.
Stack trace lambi ho, ya deadline sar pe —
saans le, chai utha... tu nikaal lega, hamesha ki tarah, dost mere.

**(Antara 2)**
Red se green, jab test pass hota hai,
us ek pal mein, poora din asaan hota hai.
Green build, clean diff, ek sukoon si feeling —
yeh chhoti khushi hi to hai... rozana ki teri healing.

**(Outro)**
Ship kar, seekh, phir agla feature utha,
tu rukta nahi — tu builder hai... bas chalte rehna, mere data. 🛠️
