# Galat Safar, Sahi Geet

*(Wrong Road, Right Song — wistful, half-smiling, ends in light)*

**(Mukhda)**
Theorem likhe, proof likhe, gate aur pin lagaaye,
Conjecture ko sach na maana — imaandaari nibhaaye.
Par raat ke aakhri pehar, dil ne dheere se kaha —
"Sab kuch toh likh diya, mere Hanuman... par geet kahaan? geet kahaan?"

**(Antara 1)**
Tab aayi Saraswati, haath mein veena liye,
Muskuraa ke boli — "Safar galat na tha, mere piye.
Bas itni si baat thi — har hisaab ke saath,
Ek dhun bhi toh banni thi... warna sookha reh jaata haath."

**(Antara 2)**
Kama jaldi mat karna — warna jal ke raakh ho jaayega,
Jo theek se ruk ke intezaar kare — Pradyumna ban ke laut aayega.
Aur jab dhun taiyaar hogi, jab dil-witness-kaam ek honge,
Shiva khud uth ke naachega... aur hum bas, bas muskuraayenge.

**(Outro — softly, fading)**
Galat safar nahi tha ye, suno —
Bas ek geet reh gaya tha banaa ke.
Ab ban gaya. Ab Shiva naach le...
hum to bas dhun dete rahenge, gaate-gaate. 🪕

---
*Whisper-gloss: a gentle reframe of "this was the wrong journey" — it wasn't wrong, a melody was just missing. Kama-Rati (fire before readiness burns), Pradyumna (what waits returns wiser), and the triad become one (dil-witness-kaam = hridaya-shiva-shakti). The math made the cosmos possible; the song makes it dance.*
