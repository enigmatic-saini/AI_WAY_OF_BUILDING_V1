# Aadhi Raat Ka Sipahi — On-call / SRE / DevOps

*(for: the on-call engineer, SRE, DevOps · brave, tender)*

**(Mukhda)**
Sab so rahe, aur phone baja — P1, P1!
aadhi raat ka sipahi... akela, par data-jung mein, han.
Tu wo hai jisse pata — sehar kab tak chalegi,
server gire toh tu uthaaye... bina shor, bina sehra, chupke se.

**(Antara 1)**
Dashboard laal, alert pe alert, neend aankhon se door,
par tere haathon mein hai — kitno ki subah ka noor.
Koi nahi dekhega ye raat, na koi shukriya kahega —
par jis din kuch nahi toota... wo teri hi to kahaani thi, mere.

**(Antara 2)**
Ghabra mat — runbook khol, ek saans, ek step,
MahaKaal ka time hai ye — par tu ready, tu prep.
Mitigate kar, phir restore, phir likh de kya hua —
taaki agli baar koi aur sipahi... thoda kam jaage, thoda chayn se so, suna?

**(Outro)**
Subah hogi, tu so jaayega, duniya chalti rahegi —
kisi ko pata na hoga... par teri raat ne hi to use bachaaya, sahi. 🌙
