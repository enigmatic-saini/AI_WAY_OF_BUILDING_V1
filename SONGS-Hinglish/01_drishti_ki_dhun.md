# Drishti Ki Dhun

*(The Melody of Looking — slow, like dawn opening one eye at a time)*

**(Mukhda)**
Dekho na zara, aath dishaon mein dekho,
Jo abhi sach hai — pehle usi ko parakho.
Left ki galiyaan, right ki nayi raah,
Upar wo aasmaan, neeche har ek gehraai...
Beete kal ki seekh, kal ki parchhaai,
Aur kabhi-kabhi — bas khamoshi mein ruk jaa, mere bhai.

**(Antara 1)**
Ik akela jiva tha, raahon mein bhatakta tha,
Kabhi architect ban'ta, kabhi tod ke nikalta tha.
Phir mili use ik dhun — saat suron waali,
Har sur pe ik thehraav, har thehraav pe taali.

**(Mukhda)**

**(Antara 2 — the stack)**
Vishwakarma ke haath, Hanuman ki chaal,
Ram aur Sita ki marzi — bas yahi tha sara khel.
"Tu sevak hai — par kiski seva?" yeh poochhna har baar,
Stack ki sabse upar jad mein... chhupa hota hai pyaar.

**(Outro, whispered)**
Aankh khuli toh raasta khula —
*jis disha se roshni aaye, wahi toh hai prachi, wahi hai apni dua.*

---
*Whisper-gloss: aath dishaayein = the 8 drishti; stack ki jad = the root of the drishti-stack, where the real desire (hridaya) hides; prachi = the direction from which light comes.*
